Interviewing a Street Fighter Fanatic

February 2018 | Digital Video | Adobe Premiere Pro

A video in which I interview a friend to get to the bottom of why he loves playing a game that's full of toxic people. Why continue to invest time and energy on a game that can be emotionally/physically draining and is full of trash-talking, elitist players? Maybe the answers will give insights to other players' mindsets in different games.
